Community Action Marin

Community Action Marin strives for everyone in Marin to achieve well being by getting educational, health, and other essential servies. They cover financial emergencies, they provide child care and early childhood education, they help people manage their mental health, and so much more. They are willing to help anyone along their road to properity.
