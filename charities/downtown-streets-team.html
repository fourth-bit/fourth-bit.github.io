Downtown Streets Team

The Downtown Streets Team help homeless people in Marin and other areas. They treat the people who they help with dignity and they make them feel empowered. Those who they help work on projects with them. The Downtown Streets Team's ultimate goal is to transition the people who the help into empoyment and having a job. They believe that having a job restores hope and creates opportunity, so the Downtown Streets Team helps homeless people do this.
