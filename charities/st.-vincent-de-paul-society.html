St. Vincent de Paul Society

The St. Vincent de Paul Society strives to give care and important services to people who are struggling in Marin County. Their main programs include, the free dining room, the homeless outreach team, and the jobs stipend and employment training program. They believe that everyone deserves food, housing, and a chance for a better life.
