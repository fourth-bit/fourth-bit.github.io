SCRAP SF

SCRAP is a creative reuse center in San Francisco. They put new life into craft supplies, and they annually divert 200 tons of materials from the landfill. SCRAP is a place for the community to find materials for educational programs, creative projects, and their classrooms. They deliver underprivileged students art kits and instruction, and so much more.
